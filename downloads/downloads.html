Baptist Missions OS is a specialized Linux distribution built upon the stability of MX Linux and the flexibility of Arch Linux (MXArch). Our mission is to provide Baptist missionaries with a reliable, secure, and pre-configured operating system that includes all the tools necessary for their ministry.

Key Features:

  • Pre-configured Tools: Includes Bible study software, office suites, and communication tools.
  • Security: Built with privacy and security in mind for missionaries operating in sensitive areas.
  • Stability: Based on proven MX Linux and Arch Linux foundations.
  • Lightweight: Optimized to run on a wide range of hardware, including older laptops.
